Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

1. Information We Process

Brewlore Studio may process basic TikTok account information authorized by the user, such as account identification data returned through TikTok Login Kit.

Brewlore Studio may also process video files and captions selected by the user for upload to TikTok through TikTok's official Content Posting API.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used only to connect the user's TikTok account, display the connected account, and upload user-selected videos to TikTok drafts or inbox.

3. Data Sharing

Brewlore Studio does not sell user data. Data is shared with TikTok only as needed to perform user-authorized login and content upload actions.

4. Data Storage

Brewlore Studio stores only the information necessary to operate the upload workflow. Access tokens and uploaded content should be handled securely and removed when no longer needed.

5. User Control

Users may disconnect Brewlore Studio from their TikTok account through TikTok account settings or by contacting us.
